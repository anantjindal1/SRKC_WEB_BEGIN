About Us

We would like to introduce ourselves as SHREE RAM KRIPA CONSTRUCTIONS, an “AA” Class “CIVIL CONTRACTOR”, started in 2007, working in Infrastructure Sector. We are specialised in Roads ,Railways, Highway, RCC structures like Railway Over Bridges, Major Bridges & Underpasses. We have also executed Building projects.

We have worked with well reputed Private and Government organisations. Here are a few names : L&T, Punj Lloyd Ltd, Jaipur Development Authority, North Western Railways, North Central Railways, West Central Railways, IVRCL Infra , Rajasthan PWD, RIDCOR Ltd, RIDCOR Infra Projects Ltd.

Recent Projects

We have Completed Three Bridge works with M/S IVRCL, Three Railway Over Bridge and Four Mega Highway projects with RIDCOR, Two Major Bridge works with North Western Railways (NWR), Two River Bridge with PWD Rajasthan, Two Bridges with Jaipur Development Authority Many Minor Bridge works with RIDCOR, Three Bridge Projects with (NWR), among a number of Underpasses and other structures.
